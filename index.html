How it works.

Every week, you'll get an email.

The email will summarize some of Donald Trump's tweets that week.

But instead of leaving you hopeless and not sure how to take action, these tweets will be paired with links to donate to great non-profits working on the issues he's tweeting about.

That way, you can help offset what he's doing while you read his tweets.
